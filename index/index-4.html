故事簡介

中世紀的維京人是當時號稱表最強的戰鬥民族，他們以殘暴的手段襲擊歐陸。一位傳說中的戰士托爾茲的兒子托爾芬，父親死後，因為復仇的關係被迫在殘酷的戰場上長大，跟著殺父仇人阿謝拉德四處征戰，找尋著夢幻的大地「文蘭」。

Around the end of the millennium, Viking, the mightiest but atrocious men, had been outbreaking in everywhere. Thorfinn, the son of the greatest warrior, lived his childhood in a battlefield. He was seeking a land of reverie called Vinland. This is the story of a true warrior in an age of turmoil.
